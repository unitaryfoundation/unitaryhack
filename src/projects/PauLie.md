The control of quantum systems lies at the heart of quantum computation and is studied via the dynamical Lie algebra (DLA), i.e. the Lie algebra that is generated by terms in the system's Hamiltonian. The main feature of paulie is the efficient DLA classification of systems that are generated by Pauli strings based on the anticommutation graph. Beyond that the package features various quantum chaos and complexity quantifiers for such encoded fermionic systems.
