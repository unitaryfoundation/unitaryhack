Deltakit is the open-source toolkit for quantum error correction. Deltakit supports the full QEC workflow from circuit generation and compilation through to simulation, execution on real hardware, decoding, and post-experiment QEC analysis. New to Deltakit in 2026 are features supporting fault tolerant compilation, error budgeting, and leakage aware simulation.
